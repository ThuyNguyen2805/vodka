SoulSips-The Spirit of Vietnam, Bottled with Elegance


When tradition meets innovation, magic happens.
SoulSips is more than just infused vodka;
it’s a journey—a sip that takes you through the vibrant landscapes of Vietnam,
from tropical orchards to the bustling streets filled with the aroma of pho and banh mi.

A Sip of Vietnam in Every Bottle


When you open a bottle of SoulSips, you’re embarking on a flavorful journey that captures the heart and soul of Vietnam. Each sip tells a story—one deeply rooted in the country’s lush tropical landscapes, rich culinary heritage, and vibrant culture. The sweetness of mango is reminiscent of sun-drenched afternoons in the Mekong Delta.

The striking hue and tang of red dragon fruit evoke the dynamic energy of Vietnam’s bustling night markets, alive with colors, sounds, and scents. SoulSips doesn’t just infuse vodka with fruit; it infuses it with memories, moments, and a sense of place. The infusion process is a careful art, designed to bring out the true essence of each ingredient while maintaining the balance and smoothness of the Hanoi Vodka base.

SoulSips becomes a part of the story you’re creating.

For travelers, it’s a taste of Vietnam to take home.
For locals, it’s a celebration of the familiar flavors they hold dear. It’s a reminder of the beauty found in simple yet meaningful pleasures.


A New Era, Same Passion


SoulSips marks a new chapter in the journey that began in 2016 with Xoài Sips, the pop-up cocktail bar that delighted Saigon’s cosmopolitan crowd with its unique Vietnamese-inspired creations. What started as an experiment in blending global cocktail trends with local ingredients quickly grew into a beloved fixture of the city’s nightlife. Over the years, Xoài Sips became known for its ability to transform everyday fruits and flavors into extraordinary experiences.

With SoulSips, this passion for innovation and authenticity is taken to the next level. The transition from a vibrant bar to a bottled brand isn’t just about scaling operations; it’s about preserving the heart of what made Xoài Sips special. The same creativity, the same meticulous attention to quality, and the same desire to celebrate Vietnam’s unique identity now live in every bottle of SoulSips.

As SoulSips ventures into this new phase, its mission stays the same: to craft drinks that don’t just quench your thirst but stir your soul. It’s a testament to the idea that true passion is timeless.
